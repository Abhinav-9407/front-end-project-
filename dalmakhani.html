Dal Makhani / दाल मखनी

Ingredients

💡 Click any ingredient to check it off!

  • 1 cup Black Urad Dal (Whole Black Lentils)
  • 1/4 cup Rajma (Kidney Beans)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 cup Tomato Puree
  • 2 tbsp Butter (Makkhan)
  • 1/4 cup Fresh Cream
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Water for soaking and cooking

Method

💡 Click any step to highlight it!

  1. Soak: Wash and soak the Black Urad Dal and Rajma overnight (8-10 hours). Drain and rinse thoroughly.
  2. Cook: Pressure cook the soaked dal and rajma with 3 cups of fresh water and salt until they are extremely soft (about 6-8 whistles). Mash lightly.
  3. Sauté: In a separate pot, melt 1 tbsp butter. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Simmer: Add the tomato puree, red chili powder, and garam masala. Cook the mixture until the butter separates from the masala.
  5. Combine: Add the cooked dal mixture to the pot. Add a little water if the consistency is too thick. Bring to a gentle boil and simmer for a minimum of 20 minutes to meld the flavours.
  6. Finish: Stir in the remaining 1 tbsp butter and the fresh cream just before serving.
  7. Garnish with a drizzle of cream and fresh coriander. Serve hot with Naan or Jeera Rice.
⏰ Reminder set! Don't forget — soak your Black Urad Dal and Rajma tonight before sleeping (for 8-10 hours). You can't skip this step!! The dal will not become soft otherwise 😤
