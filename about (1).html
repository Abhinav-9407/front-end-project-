About Swaad

A digital recipe book that brings the warmth of Indian cooking to every kitchen.

Swaad (meaning "taste" in Hindi) is a beginner-friendly Indian recipe web application designed to make authentic Indian cooking accessible to everyone — from first-year hostel students to enthusiastic home cooks. The platform bridges the gap between traditional recipes and modern digital convenience.

This project was developed as part of our academic curriculum, combining our passion for food with web development skills. Swaad is more than just a recipe list — it is an interactive cooking companion that guides users step by step through each dish, making the whole experience enjoyable and stress-free.
