Potato and Cauliflower / आलू गोभी

Ingredients

💡 Click any ingredient to check it off!

  • 1 medium cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder (haldi)
  • 1 tsp coriander powder (dhania)
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Method

💡 Click any step to highlight it while cooking!

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add onions and sauté until light golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger and turmeric powder. Cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add the tomato puree, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates from the masala (about 5-7 minutes).
  5. Add the potatoes and cauliflower florets. Mix well to coat all the vegetables with the spice blend.
  6. Cover the pan and cook on medium-low heat for 15-20 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender. Stir occasionally.
  7. Sprinkle the remaining garam masala over the curry and cook for another 2 minutes.
  8. Garnish generously with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with roti or rice.
