Kadhai Paneer / कढ़ाई पनीर

Ingredients

For Kadhai Masala (Whole Spices):

💡 Click to check off!

  • 1 tbsp Coriander Seeds
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tsp Fennel Seeds (Saunf)
  • 3-4 Dry Red Chilies

For Curry:

  • 200g Paneer (Cottage Cheese), cubed
  • 1 large Onion, diced into cubes
  • 1 large Bell Pepper (Capsicum), diced into cubes
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 3 medium Tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup Tomato Puree
  • 2 tbsp Oil or Ghee
  • Salt and Garam Masala to taste

Method

💡 Click any step to highlight it!

  1. Make Masala: Dry roast all 'Kadhai Masala' ingredients in a pan until fragrant. Let cool, then grind coarsely. Set aside.
  2. Prep Veggies: Heat 1 tbsp oil/ghee in a heavy-bottomed pan (Kadhai). Sauté the diced onions and bell peppers for 3-4 minutes until slightly soft but still crunchy. Remove and set aside.
  3. Cook Gravy: Add 1 tbsp oil/ghee to the same pan. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1 minute.
  4. Stir in the tomato purees. Cook until the oil separates, indicating the tomatoes are fully cooked.
  5. Spices: Add the prepared Kadhai Masala, salt, and garam masala. Mix well and cook for 2 minutes, adding a splash of water if needed.
  6. Combine: Add the sautéed onions and peppers back to the pan. Add the cubed paneer.
  7. Mix gently so the paneer is coated with the spice mixture. Cook for 5 minutes until heated through.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh coriander, alongside Roti or Naan.
